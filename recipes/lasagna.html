Classic Italian Lasagna

Layered to perfection, this classic Italian lasagna features rich meat sauce, creamy cheese, and a touch of fresh basil.


Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • A few fresh basil leaves
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 9 lasagna sheets
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook onions until soft.
  2. Add garlic and ground beef. Cook until browned.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, pepper, and oregano.
  4. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, and half the mozzarella.
  6. Boil lasagna sheets if required by the package.
  7. In a baking dish, layer: meat sauce, lasagna sheets, and cheese mixture.
  8. Repeat until full.
  9. Top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan.
  10. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for about 35-40 minutes.
  11. Garnish with fresh basil and let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
